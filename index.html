Reading the matchup: their Fire has 3f startup. Dash through the bullet, and they're stuck in 8f recovery — your free punish. Land a hit and they freeze in hit-stun (26f, decaying each hit) — enough to Fire again for a combo, but the freeze shrinks every hit so combos cap out instead of looping forever.

⚡ Adrenaline: below 45% HP your speed & fire rate ramp up, reaching 1.5x at 1% HP — a comeback window when you're nearly dead.

⚡ Charge: hold Space to charge a blast. Gold ring = fully charged (2s cap). Release to fire — bigger, faster, harder-hitting the longer you charge. You can't move while charging; tap a direction to freeze & bank the charge (dashed ring) — it fires the next time you press Space. Getting hit won't cancel your charge.
