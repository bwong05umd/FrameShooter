⚡ Mechanics

Charge: hold Space, release to fire — bigger & harder the longer you hold (gold ring = full, 2s). You can't move while charging; tap a direction to bank the charge (dashed ring) for your next Space. Getting hit won't cancel it.

Adrenaline: below 45% HP your speed & fire rate ramp up to 1.5× near death — a comeback window.

Cancels: the last few recovery frames flow into a follow-up — fire→fire rapid-fires, parry→fire punishes.

🎁 Power-ups

Triple — 3 parallel shots, fires 2×
Sniper — slim, fast, lethal, fires 1×
A capsule streaks in and bounces around center. Shoot it to collect, then press K to load it.
